Python Demonstrator

Instructed Python tutorials for groups of 40+ engineering students, supporting their development in programming fundamentals and problem-solving. I provided hands-on coding help, clarified core concepts, and adapted explanations for students with varying levels of experience.

The role demanded clear technical articulation and the ability to tailor answers in real time, ensuring every student left with a stronger understanding of both the “how” and the “why” of programming.

Doing so has strengthened my problem-solving by requiring quick, clear answers to not usually expected questions, and has also sharpened my communication and adaptability through forcing me to translate complex ideas into explanations that worked for different learning styles.
